# TechCorp SaaS - Company Profile

## Overview
TechCorp SaaS is a leading provider of cloud-based project management and collaboration tools for remote teams. Our flagship product, "TeamFlow", helps organizations streamline workflows, manage tasks, and facilitate real-time communication.

## Mission
To empower distributed teams to achieve their best work through intuitive, reliable, and secure collaboration software.

## Target Audience
-   Small to Medium-sized Businesses (SMBs)
-   Remote-first companies
-   Agile development teams
-   Marketing agencies

## Core Values
1.  **Simplicity:** Our tools should be easy to adopt.
2.  **Transparency:** Open communication within teams.
3.  **Reliability:** 99.99% uptime guarantee.
4.  **Customer Success:** We grow when our customers grow.

## Pricing Tiers
-   **Starter:** Free for up to 5 users. Basic task management.
-   **Pro:** $12/user/month. Unlimited boards, timeline view, custom fields.
-   **Business:** $25/user/month. Advanced security, SSO, workload management.
-   **Enterprise:** Custom pricing. Dedicated support, audit logs, unlimited automation.
