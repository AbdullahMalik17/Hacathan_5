# TeamFlow - Product Documentation

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Account Creation
To create an account, visit https://app.teamflow.com/signup. You can sign up using email or Google SSO.

### 1.2 Workspace Setup
After signing up, you will be prompted to create a Workspace. A Workspace is the home for your team's projects.
1.  Click "Create Workspace".
2.  Enter your team name.
3.  Invite team members via email.

## 2. Features

### 2.1 Boards
Boards are where tasks live. You can choose between Kanban, List, or Calendar views.
-   **Creating a Board:** Click "+" in the sidebar.
-   **Lists:** Columns on a board (e.g., To Do, Doing, Done).
-   **Cards:** Individual tasks. Click a card to add details.

### 2.2 Tasks (Cards)
Cards can contain:
-   Descriptions (Markdown supported)
-   Assignees
-   Due Dates
-   Attachments (up to 25MB per file on Pro)
-   Checklists

### 2.3 Automations (Pro & Business)
Automate repetitive tasks.
-   *Trigger:* When card is moved to "Done".
-   *Action:* Mark due date as complete and notify manager.

### 2.4 Integrations
TeamFlow integrates with:
-   **Slack:** Receive notifications in Slack channels.
-   **GitHub:** Link PRs to tasks.
-   **Google Drive:** Attach files directly.

## 3. Account Management

### 3.1 Password Reset
Go to Settings > Security > Change Password. If logged out, click "Forgot Password" on the login screen.

### 3.2 Billing
Billing is managed by Workspace Admins in Settings > Billing.
-   We accept Visa, Mastercard, and Amex.
-   Invoices are sent monthly to the billing email.

### 3.3 Deleting Account
To delete your account, contact support. This is a permanent action.

## 4. Troubleshooting

### 4.1 Login Issues
-   Clear browser cache.
-   Ensure cookies are enabled.
-   Check status.teamflow.com for outages.

### 4.2 Mobile App
Available on iOS and Android. Search "TeamFlow" in the App Store or Play Store.
